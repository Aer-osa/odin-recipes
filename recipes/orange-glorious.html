Orange Glorious

This is a rip off recipe of DQ's Orange Julious drink. Easy to make if you have a blender.

Ingredients

  • 1 (6 ounce) can frozen orange juice concentrate
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup water
  • Â½ cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 12 cubes ice

Directions

  1. In a blender, combine orange juice concentrate, milk, water, sugar and vanilla. Add ice cubes and blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.
